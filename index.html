Cafe Trivia

When did Cafes first appeared in India?

Coffee was first introduced to India by Baba Budan, a Sufi saint, in the 17th century. According to legend, Baba Budan smuggled seven coffee beans from Yemen to India and planted them in the hills of Chikmagalur in Karnataka, which is now known as Baba Budangiri.
